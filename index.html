About Us

Description: Curious? Read this and you'll be able to know us better

The Club of Programmers (COPS) is a student organization dedicated to promoting the study and practice of computer programming. We were established in 2016 by a group of senior high school ICT students who were passionate about programming.

We host a variety of events throughout the year, including programming contests, movie showings, and game events. We also offer mentorship programs to help students learn new skills and develop their programming talents.

Our members come from all different backgrounds, but we are all united by our love of programming. We believe that programming is a powerful tool that can be used to solve problems, create new things, and make the world a better place.

What we do

Description: you know.... we do lots of stuff

  • Host programming contests: We host a variety of programming contests throughout the year, including local, regional, and national competitions. These contests provide students with the opportunity to compete against their peers and test their skills.

  • Movie showings: We show a variety of movies related to computer science and programming. These movies provide students with inspiration and motivation to pursue a career in programming.

  • Game events (eSports): We host a variety of game events, including tournaments, LAN parties, and game jams. These events provide students with the opportunity to compete in games, learn new skills, and make new friends.

  • Mentorship programs: We offer mentorship programs to help students learn new skills and develop their programming talents. These programs are led by experienced programmers who are passionate about helping others.

Vision & Mission

Description: What's it for us in the future?

Vision: To provide COPS members with sufficient skills to find solutions for in-house needs, such as timetable creation, duty allocation etc. To encourage students to propose their innovative ideas to the world through their participation in various coding events and bringing laurels to the institution. To encourage students should develop their own mobile apps and thereby turn into young entrepreneurs. To improve students design skills. To encourage students to develop as innovative beings, transforming them into confident and competent users.

Mission: To inculcate passion for coding among the students and to show them how easy it is to bring their ideas into reality with the usage of proper tools and software.
